Our first lesson is always free! This will be an informal lesson where we can go over your or your child’s goals for English and I get a sense of your English strength.

I offer a range of lesson types:
Basic Web Development: HTML, CSS, JavaScript & PHP
Basic, Intermediate and Advanced English Reading, Writing and Conversation
Adult Lessons
Technical English: Reading, Writing, Conversation
STEM English for children and Adults
Eiken
High School Placement
College Placement
Current College Students
And custom lesson plans, too!

One on one lessons and tutoring are an hour and 15 minutes (75 minutes) and I charge an hourly rate of $30 per lesson; the price includes the cost of the lesson as well as time I invest to maintain your curriculum afterward.

We can meet at your home, a quiet public place or my place! Whatever is best for you!

If you are happy with our first few lessons, we can work out a package plan!
