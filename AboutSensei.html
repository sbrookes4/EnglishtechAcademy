About Shawna
シャーナ先生
について

I have three main passions: technology, Japan and teaching. I'm combining these passions for the local Portland Japanese community who want to learn and improve their English!

I lived in Japan for 3 years and was the head teacher at a traditional all-Japanese kindergarten. There I created year long curriculums for all ages of students including Reading, Writing, Listening, Conversation and Mathematics. I also taught as an ALT for Elementary and Jr. High School students as well as taught adult and corporate English lessons. I lived in Ibaraki and Tochigi Prefectures and have traveled throughout Japan, from Kagoshima to Hakodate.

I made the effort to learn Japanese because my personal philosophy is that if I understand the Japanese language as well as the difficulties of being an ESL student, that will help me be a better teacher.

My favorite places in Japan are Hiroshima, Kagoshima, Oknouin, Yamadera and Akihabara. My favorite food is Okonomiyaki. I don’t have a favorite kanji but I do like to write kanji that have the ⻌, 廴 and 广 radicals. Japanese grapes are the best!

Teaching English in Japan, I always gave my 120%!

I truly miss Japan and think of Japan everyday.

Teaching English was the best job I ever had and it made me happy. I loved living in Japan,too. It was heartbreaking to have to return to America; but dreams never die -
いつか私は日本にもどる。
