English Technical Academy in Portland, Oregon is an English Juku for Japanese of all ages and levels.

ETA offers basic English language tutoring and conversation lessons but also provides targeted emphasis in “STEM” (Science, Technology, Engineering & Math) education for students in the hard sciences.

Whether you have a child you wish to become bilingual in STEM English or if you are a fluent English speaker in a technical field that wants to improve your language skills, English Technical Academy can help you!
