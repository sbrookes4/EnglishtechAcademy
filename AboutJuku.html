About
塾について

English Technical Academy teaches basic English lessons but has a niche specialty in technical English!

English Technical Academy has a long term vision for having Jukus in Portland and in Japan. While in Japan, Shawna Sensei saw the need for an English school that teaches both traditional English classes and STEM (Science, technology, Engineering & Math) oriented English for Japanese students of all ages. Shawna is pursuing this vision and is accepting students to build her Juku.

English Technical Academy is an innovative English-Japanese school and one of its unique subjects is 3D Printing.

The 21st Century will be a century of rapid technological advancement and traditional teaching methods need some modernization; after seeing first-hand how Japanese students can learn both Japanese and English at the same time and realizing that if children are exposed to STEM early on, Japanese children can “grow up” learning English STEM. English technical Academy wants to see your child be prepared for the 21st Century. If you are an adult in college or here in America working, I can strengthen your English skills.

My method is to evaluate each student, make a curriculum tailored for their needs and track their progress. I always welcome your feedback and if you feel we need to adjust the curriculum, I will do so.

I am passionate about education, the pursuit of knowledge and empowering people and I truly want all my students to succeed!

Babies &
Children
赤ちゃん・
子供たち

For babies and little children, I like to use cute, bright books that stimulate their senses. With such books, letters, numbers, colors, shapes and vocabulary can be learned quickly.

In Japan, I got great results by teaching both English and Japanese in the same lesson. Children have an incredible capacity to learn English and Japanese side-by-side - the old mindset of "students will get confused if taught at the same time" is not true. In reality, the child's mind is in the formative years with great neuroplasticity and when the children learn side-by-side, the English-Japanese relationship forms solidly at a young age.

STEM

For STEM English, I can start your child learning Science, Technology, Engineering and Math at a very young age. It’s never too early for children to learn STEM vocabulary – “start them out smart and young!” I believe if you start a child at a “higher” level, they will be able to excel quicker and will turn out to be brilliant young adults. The 21st Century will need talented, smart, problem-solving people and when a child “grows up with STEM” they will be the scientists, engineers and technicians the world will need.

Teaching STEM is my passion and I want to be a teacher spreads seeds of knowledge that will one day sprout and make the world a greener, safer, happier and peaceful place.
